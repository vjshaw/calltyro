Frequently Asked Questions

Is Calltyro safe for children?

Yes. All calls are AI-generated educational content only. We never collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can monitor all activity through our parent dashboard.

Do you need parental permission?

Yes, students under 18 require parental consent to use Callty. Parents must create the account and can monitor all learning activities and progress.

What information do you collect?

We only collect necessary information: email, phone number for calls, and grade level for appropriate content. We never share personal data and comply with COPPA and privacy laws.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts or cancellation fees. Cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings or by contacting support.

How do the AI calls work?

Our AI creates educational conversations based on curriculum standards. Calls are completely automated, safe, and focused only on learning topics appropriate for your grade level.

What if my child needs help?

Parents have full access to call history, progress reports, and can set restrictions. Our support team is available to help families get the most from Callty.
