Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [DATE]

Important for Parents: Calltyro is committed to protecting children's privacy. We comply with COPPA and require parental consent for users under 13.

1. Information We Collect

For Users Under 13:

  • With Parental Consent Only: Email address (parent's), phone number for calls, grade level
  • Educational Data: Call history, learning progress, topics studied
  • We Do NOT Collect: Full names, addresses, personal details, or any unnecessary information

For Users 13-17:

  • With Parental Consent: Email, phone number, grade level, basic progress tracking
  • Parents maintain full control and access to all account data

For Adult Users (18+):

  • Email address and phone number for service delivery
  • Learning preferences and progress data
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

2. How We Use Information

  • Educational Calls: To deliver personalized learning conversations
  • Progress Tracking: To show learning improvement over time
  • Safety: To ensure appropriate content for grade level
  • Communication: To send important account updates (with parent approval for minors)

3. Information Sharing

We NEVER sell, rent, or share personal information. Your data stays with Callty.

Limited Sharing Only For:

  • Service Providers: Twilio (for calls), Stripe (for payments) - all COPPA compliant
  • Legal Requirements: Only if required by law
  • Safety: To protect children or prevent harm

4. Parental Rights (COPPA)

Parents have the right to:

  • Review all information collected from their child
  • Delete their child's account and all data
  • Refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Access call history and learning progress
  • Set restrictions on call times and topics

5. Data Security

  • All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers with limited access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No data stored longer than necessary

COPPA Compliance Statement

Child Protection First: Calltyro strictly complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Our COPPA Commitments

1. Parental Consent

  • Required Before Collection: We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13
  • Consent Methods: Email verification plus phone confirmation with parent
  • Clear Disclosure: Parents receive full details about what information we collect and how we use it

2. Limited Data Collection

We collect only what's necessary for the educational service:
  • Parent's email address (for account management)
  • Child's phone number (for educational calls only)
  • Grade level (for appropriate content)
  • Learning progress (to track improvement)

3. No Unnecessary Information

We do NOT collect:

  • Full names or addresses
  • Photos or videos
  • Social security numbers
  • Location data beyond phone number
  • Browsing history or cookies from children

4. Parental Control Rights

Parents can always:

  • Review all information we have about their child
  • Delete their child's account and all data
  • Refuse further data collection
  • Modify account settings and restrictions
  • Contact us with questions or concerns

5. Safe Sharing Practices

  • No Selling: We never sell children's information
  • Limited Sharing: Only with COPPA-compliant service providers (Twilio for calls)
  • Secure Storage: All data encrypted and securely stored

Child Safety & Protection

Safety First: Your child's safety is our highest priority. Every aspect of Callty is designed with child protection in mind.

How We Keep Children Safe

1. Content Safety

  • Educational Only: All content is curriculum-based and age-appropriate
  • No Personal Questions: AI never asks for personal information during calls
  • Grade-Level Appropriate: Content automatically matched to child's grade level
  • Monitored Conversations: All calls follow pre-approved educational scripts

2. Communication Safety

Safe Communication Practices:
  • Only educational phone calls - no chat or messaging
  • Calls during daytime hours only (8 AM - 8 PM)
  • Parents can set restricted call times
  • No personal information sharing during calls

3. Technical Safety

  • Secure Platform: All calls encrypted and secure
  • No Downloads: No software or apps for children to download
  • Limited Access: Only authorized phone numbers can receive calls
  • Automatic Limits: Call duration limits to prevent overuse

4. Parental Oversight

Parents Always Have:

  • Full access to call history and topics discussed
  • Ability to set call time restrictions
  • Control over which subjects are available
  • Option to listen to call recordings (where legally permitted)
  • Immediate ability to pause or cancel service

5. Reporting & Support

If You Have Concerns:
  • Contact us immediately at safety@calltyro.com
  • We investigate all safety reports within 24 hours
  • Emergency contact: 1-214-506-8543

Red Flags to Report

Contact us immediately if:

  • Your child reports inappropriate questions or content
  • Calls occur outside scheduled times
  • Your child seems uncomfortable after a call
  • You notice any technical issues or security concerns
